## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ Accordion)

*   **Q: What is a DopeKin digital twin?**
    *   *A*: A digital twin is a high-fidelity AI replication of a creator's voice, personality, and visual appearance. They can chat, voice call via FaceTime, and broadcast live streams autonomously based on the creator's uploaded cognitive data.
*   **Q: How do I train my own twin?**
    *   *A*: Go to the Creator Studio, fill in basic bio details, upload 5 photos (from different angles) for visual mapping, record 10 seconds of your voice for vocal cloning, upload your documents (e.g. scripts, blogs) for cognitive training, set your pricing model, and activate.
*   **Q: Is the voice synthesis real-time?**
    *   *A*: Yes. When you call a twin via FaceTime, their voice is synthesized on-the-fly using advanced low-latency text-to-speech technology, providing a natural conversation flow.
*   **Q: How do subscriptions work?**
    *   *A*: Some twins require a monthly subscription fee to unlock FaceTime calling and premium chat access. Free twins are open to all users for basic text chat.
